Got Questions? ⚽

Everything you need to know before booking!

Anna trains kids ages 4–10. Whether your child is just starting out or looking to level up their skills, she's got you covered!
Private 1-on-1 sessions are $14/hour. Small group sessions (2–4 kids) are $10/hour per kid. Great value for awesome training!
All sessions are held in Edina parks and fields — including Braemar Field. We come to you, so no travel stress!
Light rain? We can still train! Heavy rain or storms? We'll reschedule for a day that works for both of us — no problem at all.
We accept cash or parent-approved digital payments like Venmo. Details will be shared after your first booking!
